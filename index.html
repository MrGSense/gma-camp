About Janet

I was appointed to the IRB City Commission in November, 2023 and want to continue to serve the residents of IRB. I have been part of the IRB community since 1996:. First as a vacationer, in 1999 as a vacation condo owner, as snowbirds in 2016, then full-time residents in 2019. My personal and professional experiences have provided me a set of skills I feel make me uniquely suited to the City Commission position. As a school administrator I developed skills in group presentation, grant writing, program development, community partnerships, and addressing legislative bodies. As a citizen-led committee co-chair; I presented to community organizations and city government, wrote editorials, and appeared in media. As a special need parent, I served on state advisory boards charged with program development.

As city commisioner I will continue to work on these issues in our local community

  • STR Regulation
  • Beach Preservation
  • Support for Small Local Businesses
  • Support of Community Organizations & Events
  • Safety
  • Parking
  • Drainage
  • Erosion of Local Governmental Control
